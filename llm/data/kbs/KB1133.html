How do I get legal aid to defend criminal charges made against me?

Last checked: 16 June 2023

Apply for legal aid as soon as you receive summons

If you decide to apply for legal aid, do it as soon as you have been charged or receive a summons to go to court. Do not wait until your first day in court.
